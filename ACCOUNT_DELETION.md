# Account Deletion — PeakPilot

To request deletion of your PeakPilot account and all associated data:

**In the app:**
1. Open PeakPilot and go to the **Profile** tab
2. Scroll to the **Session** section
3. Tap **"Delete Account"**
4. A confirmation will appear: *"This will permanently delete your account and all your data. This cannot be undone."*
5. Tap **"Delete Forever"** to confirm

Your account and data are deleted immediately upon confirmation.

**Alternatively, by email:**
If you're unable to access the app, email paulchamp1602@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account", sent from the email address associated with your account.

## What gets deleted
- Account details (email, display name)
- Workout history and streak data
- Saved favourite workouts
- Weekly AI plans

Deletion is immediate and permanent — there is no recovery period.
