# Account Deletion — PeakPilot

To request deletion of your PeakPilot account and all associated data:

**In the app:**
1. Open PeakPilot
2. Go to [Profile tab]
3. Tap [Settings/menu icon]
4. Tap "Delete Account"
5. Confirm when prompted

**Or by email:**
Email paulchamp1602@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account", sent from the email address associated with your account.

## What gets deleted
- Account details (email, display name)
- Workout history and streak data
- Saved favourite workouts
- Weekly AI plans

Account deletion is processed immediately upon confirmation in the app, or within a reasonable timeframe for email requests.
